Commercial

Tower Electric, Inc realizes that you are only as good as your field and our product is defined as to how we build a project for one of our clients. Having the best employees within the market to achieve the status of a top quality electrical contractor is a priority. Our field personnel must have the knowledge of our industry to ensure that a project is built with superior craftsmanship and to the project specifications. All of our employees receive continuing education and training to understand today's codes and proper installations. Our employees from the project management level, to the first year apprentice, are infused into proper planning, layout, pre-fabrication and quality control on every project we do. The overall project safety, from monthly site inspections, weekly training and daily job site awareness, is priority #1 for our employees . Tower Electric has continued to bring projects together on time, within budget, building the reputation within our market as a top Electrical Contractor.

Industrial

Industrial electrical installations are typically not your average install and require a different level of knowledge to complete the scope of work. Extensive coordination and understanding of the individual equipment is required for proper control and power connections.

Building Controls

Given the opportunities and partnerships within our market, Building Controls has been a instrumental part of the development of our company and no doubt an exciting time for Tower Electric. We are able to work hand in hand with the General Contractor and Mechanical subcontractors to provide additional services to a project. This area of work has allowed us to extend another area of expertise under our excellent workmanship to ensure a complete project is delivered to the end user.

Medium Voltage

With many projects in today's market, Medium Voltage work is becoming yet another branch in the complete service we provide to our clients. We have the capabilities to install and service this area of work to our clients.
